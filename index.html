MonsterGolf JS

Objective:

The monster is HANGRY! Time your swings to get the food to the monster in as few strokes as possible.

Controls:

  • UP/DOWN/LEFT/RIGHT (W/S/A/D): Swing direction.
  • HOLD SHIFT: More granular swing-direction control.
  • SPACE: Strike food.
